05 · Deck Constitution

Three operating rules for every slide. Review guardrails, not mood notes.

01
Less is more.
Every slide does exactly one job. When a slide starts doing more, cut content before adding layout. When in doubt, cut.
02
Rhythm with patterns.
Reuse established slide modes. Pattern repetition helps the viewer understand the story faster. New patterns require a genuinely different need.
03
Motion with meaning.
Motion must improve comprehension, emphasis, sequencing, or emotional timing. If the message is clearer without motion, remove it.
